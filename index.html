Mission

Our mission is to empower communities and individuals by fostering compassion, transparency, and trust. Through education, support, and ethical initiatives, we aim to create meaningful connections that uplift individuals and promote social change. We are dedicated to building a world where every person feels supported, valued, and inspired to contribute to the well-being of others.

Team

Angel Fontalvo
Founder
“Born in Manatí, Atlántico, Colombia, I grew up witnessing the struggles of those around me as they faced hardships due to a lack of resources. Whether it was for medical expenses, food, or other life emergencies, people often turned to me for help. While my heart longed to support them all, I found myself overwhelmed, unable to provide the assistance they needed due to limited resources. This experience planted the seed for what would eventually become Bridge of Support Foundation. I realized that while I couldn’t do it alone, there are others out there who share my desire to make a difference. This foundation was created with the hope of uniting compassionate individuals who are willing to extend a helping hand to those in need, creating a community of support and care that can uplift those facing life’s challenges. “
Founder's Story
